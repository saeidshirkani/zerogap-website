Our story

ZeroGap Security was founded on a simple idea: understanding how real systems fail is the key to securing them effectively.

01

Curiosity for how systems break

Everything started with analysing real-world failures — software defects, misconfigurations, overlooked assumptions, and cascading outages that revealed how fragile modern systems can be. This foundation created a mindset focused on root causes rather than surface symptoms.

02

Entering offensive security

That curiosity naturally evolved into structured learning in offensive security: penetration testing, web application security, network hardening, and the techniques used by real attackers. Understanding failure from the attacker’s point of view made the picture complete.

03

Building ZeroGap Security

The vision emerged clearly: a focused offensive security studio for Swiss SMEs and startups — offering deep manual testing, clear communication, and practical guidance without unnecessary complexity or corporate overhead.

04

Looking ahead

Today we continue refining our methodology, adopting AI only where it truly adds value, and working with a small number of clients to ensure depth, continuity, and long-term security improvement.

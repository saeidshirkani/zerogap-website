A small studio by design

ZeroGap Security is intentionally a small, focused security studio — with less overhead, fewer layers, and more attention on the systems we actually test.

Instead of anonymous rotating consultants, you work directly with the specialist performing your assessment. This ensures continuity, accountability, and a deeper understanding of your environment.

Our methodology blends curiosity-driven manual exploration with carefully selected tools and AI assistance — without turning your infrastructure into yet another automated scan.

What matters to us

We value clarity over buzzwords, realistic risk over fear, and meaningful improvement over endless reporting.

  • Transparent and honest communication about real risk
  • Helping engineers fix issues, not simply listing vulnerabilities
  • A practical balance between security, usability and delivery speed
  • Building long-term security maturity step by step

Frequently Asked Questions

Clear, short answers to the questions we receive most often.

What type of companies do you work with?

We focus on Swiss SMEs, startups and local organisations that need practical, high-impact offensive security testing without enterprise-level overhead.

Do you use AI for penetration testing?

Yes — carefully. We combine manual, human-led testing with AI-assisted analysis to speed up research, pattern recognition and reporting, without replacing real manual work.

What does a typical penetration test include?

A defined scope, deep manual testing, curated tooling, AI-assisted review, clear risk ratings and a step-by-step remediation guide.

How long does a project usually take?

Most engagements take between 1–3 weeks depending on scope, complexity and number of systems involved.

Do you offer retesting?

Yes. Once fixes are implemented, we retest the affected areas to ensure the issues are fully resolved.

Do you work remotely?

Yes. Although based in Ticino, we work with clients across all regions of Switzerland and remotely when possible.
